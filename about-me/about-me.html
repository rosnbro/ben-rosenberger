About Me

I’m Ben.

I’ve been taking film photos since early 2017. I was drawn to film as a means of learning how photography works. I stayed for the aesthetic.

I write what I feel. Whether it be an essay, review, poem, or book. I take vivid images in my head and try to paint them with words. It takes me a long time.

I graduated from Virginia Tech with a degree in mechanical engineering.

I love to travel. Maybe I’ll see you around.

Until then.
